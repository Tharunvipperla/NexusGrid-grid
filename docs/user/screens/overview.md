# Overview

**Sidebar → My node → Overview.** The "Cluster overview" dashboard — your node's
health and activity at a glance, and the fastest way to see the whole grid is
working.

---

## Key metrics

Top-line counters:
- **Running now** — tasks executing right now.
- **Queue** — tasks waiting to be scheduled.
- **Peers online** — how many connected peers are currently reachable.
- **Hosting for peers** — how much foreign-storage you're holding for others.

## Happening now / Insights

- **Happening now** — a live activity feed of what your node is doing (dispatches,
  transfers, grants…).
- **Insights** — derived observations about your node and grid (capacity, balance
  of give/receive, anything worth your attention).

---

## Try it: hello-world dispatch

There's a one-click button to **dispatch a built-in hello-world task and watch the
full loop** — nothing to prepare. It's the quickest way to confirm your node can
schedule, run, and report a task end-to-end. The result shows up in
[Task Telemetry](telemetry.md).

This is a great first thing to click after install.
